We lead strategic conservation efforts to protect Uganda's most vital ecosystems through indigenous reforestation, species protection, and local partnership.

Pillar One

Indigenous Reforestation in the Albertine Rift

The Albertine Rift is one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth. We are restoring over 5,000 hectares of degraded land by planting native tree species that stabilize the soil and provide natural carbon sequestration.

  • check_circle 50,000+ Native saplings planted annually
  • check_circle Carbon credit initiatives for local communities

Pillar Three

Community-Led Stewardship

We believe true conservation happens at the kitchen table. By empowering local farmers with regenerative agricultural tools and education, we turn forest edge communities into its most fierce protectors.

"When we protect the forest, the forest protects our harvest. It's a new era for our village."
