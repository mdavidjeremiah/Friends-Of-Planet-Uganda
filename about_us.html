Preserving the Heart of Africa

A legacy of restoration, conservation, and community-led environmental stewardship in the Pearl of Africa.

Our Mandate

To create a sustainable future for Uganda where nature and people thrive together.

MISSION

Protecting Uganda's biodiversity through scientific research and grassroots action.

VISION

A nation where ecological balance is the foundation of economic prosperity.

OUR HISTORY

Rooted in Local Leadership

"We did not inherit this land from our ancestors; we are borrowing it from our children."

2004

Founded by a circle of local environmentalists in Kampala, the organization began as a response to the rapid deforestation threatening the Rwenzori mountain range.

2012

Launched the 'Great Green Corridor' project, restoring over 50,000 hectares of native wetland habitats and establishing community wildlife corridors.

Today

Now a leading partner in international climate summits, we remain committed to our heritage: preservation through prestige and local empowerment.
