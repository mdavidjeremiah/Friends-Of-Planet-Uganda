Privacy Policy

Friends of Planet Uganda ("we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, volunteers, and partners. This page summarizes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and payment details when you donate, subscribe to updates, or submit a volunteer inquiry.

How we use it

We use your information to process donations, respond to inquiries, send programme updates you've opted into, and improve our conservation work. We never sell your personal data.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to info@friendsofplanetuganda.org.
